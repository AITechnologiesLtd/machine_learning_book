Sphere (1998)

reviewed by

BY EDWIN JAHIEL

SPHERE 1/2 * (half a star). Directed by Barry Levinson.Written by Stephen Hauser and Paul Attanasio, from a novel by Michael Crichton, adapted by Kurt Wimmer. Photography, Adam Greenberg. Editing,Stu Linder.Production design, Norman Reynolds. Music,Elliot Goldenthal.Produced by Levinson, Crichton and Andrew Wald.Cast: Dustin Hoffman,Sharon Stone,Samuel Jackson,Peter Coyote, Liev Schreiber.A Warner's release. 120 minutes. PG-13
